COME JOIN WITH US!

Permit us to introduce ourselves...

This church exists to serve the spiritual needs of all those who are searching for answers to the questions that really matter: "Who am I?" "Why am I here?" "What will happen after I die?" We don't have all the answers for troubling and difficult things that happen in life, but we believe that the Bible explains everything we need to know to get to heaven.

We will be happy to talk to you, study with you from the Bible or assist you in any way as you seek to learn more about God and bring your life into harmony with His will. Please don't hesitate to call on us or visit with us.

Our View of the Bible

We believe that the Bible is the inspired Word of God, revealed directly from heaven to the writers and preserved intact through the centuries (2 Timothy 3:16-17). We believe the Bible does not offer mere suggestions, nor is it outdated and irrelevant to the needs of modern society, but is timeless and applicable to all men.

Five Essentials of Salvation

FAITH changes our intellect

"They said, 'Believe in the Lord Jesus, and you will be saved, you and your household.'" - Acts 16:31 (NASB)

REPENTANCE changes our will

"Therefore repent and return, so that your sins may be wiped away, in order that times of refreshing may come from the presence of the Lord." - Acts 3:19 (NASB)

CONFESSION changes our allegiance

"that if you confess with your mouth Jesus as Lord, and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved; for with the heart a person believes, resulting in righteousness, and with the mouth he confesses, resulting in salvation." - Romans 10:9-10 (NASB)

BAPTISM changes our relationship

"Peter said to them, 'Repent, and each of you be baptized in the name of Jesus Christ for the forgiveness of your sins; and you will receive the gift of the Holy Spirit.'" - Acts 2:38 (NASB)

REMAIN FAITHFUL stay changed until death

"Do not fear what you are about to suffer. Behold, the devil is about to cast some of you into prison, so that you will be tested, and you will have tribulation for ten days. Be faithful until death, and I will give you the crown of life" - Revelation 2:10 (NASB)
